## 1. Overview  
### 1.1. Game Title  
Spaceship Horror: Synthetic Shadows  

### 1.2. Genre  
Survival Horror  

### 1.3. Platform  
PC, Consoles  

### 1.4. Target Audience  
Aged 18 and above, fans of horror games, sci-fi enthusiasts.

## 2. Game Concept  
### 2.1. Premise  
In the distant future, a spaceship is haunted by synthetic beings and organic lifeforms that have gone rogue. The player must navigate the terrifying corridors, uncovering the secrets of the ship while trying to survive.

### 2.2. Key Features  
- Dual threats from synthetics and organic monsters  
- Immersive atmospheric environments  
- Resource management and crafting system  

## 3. Mechanics  
### 3.1. Core Gameplay Loop  
- Exploration  
- Resource gathering  
- Crafting and upgrading  
- Survival against AI enemies  

### 3.2. Player Actions  
- Stealth movement  
- Combat options, ranging from melee to ranged  
- Interaction with environment for puzzles and clues.

### 3.3. Game Systems  
- AI behavior systems for synths and creatures  
- Inventory and crafting management  
- Health and sanity mechanics  

## 4. Story and Narrative  
### 4.1. Setting  
A derelict spaceship drifting in deep space.

### 4.2. Characters  
- **Protagonist:** A lone survivor with a mysterious past  
- **Antagonists:** Various synthetics and organic entities with backstories.

### 4.3. Plot Overview  
The player discovers the fate of the spaceship's crew while battling the remaining threats.

### 4.4. Themes  
- Isolation and fear  
- Man vs Machine  

## 5. Art and Sound  
### 5.1. Visual Style  
Realistic graphics with a dark and moody atmosphere.

### 5.2. Sound Design  
Dynamic audio that reacts to player actions and environmental changes.

## 6. Technical  
### 6.1. Game Engine  
Unreal Engine 5  

### 6.2. Performance Targets  
60 FPS at 1080p resolution or higher.

## 7. Project Schedule  
### 7.1. Milestones  
- **Prototype Development:** 3 Months  
- **Alpha Release:** 6 Months  
- **Beta Release:** 9 Months  
- **Final Release:** 12 Months  

### 7.2. Resources  
- Team members, software tools, asset libraries.
